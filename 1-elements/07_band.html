BLACKPINK 💕

BLACKPINK is a South Korean girl group formed by YG Entertainment. The group debuted in August 2016.
They are currently the world's most successful Korean girl group. Their fandom name is BLINK. 🫰

The members are:

  • Jisoo: Vocals
  • Jennie: Lead Vocals
  • Rosé: Main Vocals
  • Lisa: Main Dancer, Maknae
